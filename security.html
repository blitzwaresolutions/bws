Our Security Philosophy

Every product we develop integrates security at the design level. From isolation and fail-safe logic to encryption and observability, our systems are engineered to protect, monitor, and respond.

Core Security Principles

Isolation: All modules sandboxed to prevent data leakage.

Fail-Safe Logic: Critical enforcement cannot be bypassed; advisory modules fail-open.

Data Integrity: Signed updates, checksums, versioned schemas, and audit logs.

Minimal Attack Surface: Outbound-only connections, optional VPN/DNS pinning.

Privacy by Design: User data processed locally whenever possible.
